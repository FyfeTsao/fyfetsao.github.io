PROFILE

Family: Polypodiaceae
Genus: Phlebodium

LOG

2023-4-5 Bought

I met this plant when I was hanging out at local plant marketplace. They were planted in two 9cm plastic nursery pots, each charged RMB 5. That’s really cheap price. It was a special offer due to a little signs of unhealthiness compared with healthy ones which were charged RMB 15. Not supposing it to be a big problem, I happily bought them. What motivated me to buy them was not all about their appealing price – ferns are what I always want to have, but not dare to try because the climate in Beijing is known for its low humidity, which ferns definitely do not enjoy. But I just can’t resist blue star fern, it’s so beautiful. And now it’s in my houseplant collection, being the first fern.

2023-4-12 Repotted

After climating in my place for about a week, I repotted it. The root was not healthy and became pot bound, so I root-pruned it, and replaced its potting mix with my own, consisting of coconut coir, perlite and slow release fertilizers. A couple of days may be needed for it to grow again. Just be patient.

Commentary

When I first looked at the Hangul on this bottle, it immediately revived my memory of a language class years ago. The teacher from Brazil guided us how to perform an ‘a cappella’ – choral singing performed without instruments. That was the first time I knew this term. //DRAFT: A cappella,아카펠라,阿卡贝拉; cafe,카페; 빙그레에서 출시한 커피 제품.

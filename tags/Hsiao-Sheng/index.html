Hsiao Shêng

zh “小生”——中国古代文士自称的谦词。这个词也进入了汉字文化圈各国的语言中，常被列入男性第一人称的谦逊语部门之下。这个企划用以陈列一些自己的照片，以记录容姿的推移。更新可能相当随性而缓慢。

en "Hsiao Shêng" is a self address of scholars who just started in traditional China. This word was absorbed by other East Asian languages as well, listed under the category of male's humble language. The photos this project presents are all of me. You may soon discover how my physical appearance changes with the passage of time, just as I do with complex emotions.

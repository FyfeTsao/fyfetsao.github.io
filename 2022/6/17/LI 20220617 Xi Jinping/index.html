Life, Illustrated

Xi Jinping

I was reading Joseph Fewsmith’s book “Rethinking Chinese Politics” throughout May. It’s so enlightening because no one in my country has ever told me thoroughly about the affairs discussed in this book. But it’s not that unacceptable. As a man in his 30s, I understand that just like everyone has a dark side, every country has its dark past. And maybe dark present as well. This man and his zero-covid country really drives me mad.

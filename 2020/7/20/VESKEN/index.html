Funny Characters

en At first, it is the language written on the street signs and billboards that caught my eye. My interest soon expands to almost all media that language could attach to in form of written characters. Such characters – some are funny, others not – are nevertheless perfect lenses. What language is it? Who write them? What do they want by these characters? Why write like this? So many fascinating insights could be reflected through these characters in every corner.
